# The UN Security Council

The United Nations Security Council is one of the six principal organs of the UN, charged with ensuring international peace and security.

## Key Facts

- 5 permanent members (China, France, Russia, UK, USA)
- 10 non-permanent members elected for 2-year terms
- Can impose sanctions and authorize military action
